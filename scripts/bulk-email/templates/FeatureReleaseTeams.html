Hey Hacker,

We're excited to announce the release of the Teams Building feature in the SwampHacks app, available to all applicants!

By making a team, anyone can find your team by navigating to the Team Explore page in the event dashboard. From here, people can request to join your team and you can accept/deny requests on the My Team Page.

Why should you form a team before applications are due?

Forming a team prior to the application will assist us in trying to make your whole team is accepted. We are NOT guaranteeing that every team and every team member will accepted, but we do want to make sure you have the greatest chance for you to work with your team, as we want you to make your SwampHacks experience the best.

Don't have a team yet?

No worries! Every applicant will be reviewed individually, then we will consider on team basis. If you don't have team yet, don't sweat it. You still have the same chances as anyone who forms a team prior to applications due.

You can also look for potential teammates in the #looking-for-a-team channel in our Discord server.

Can I form a team after applications?

YES! You can continue to form and join teams even after the application deadline. Once application reviews begin, we won't consider any new teams formed after that date (Nov. 21st @ 11:59pm) for the application decision process.
